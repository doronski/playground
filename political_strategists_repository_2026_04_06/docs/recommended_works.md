## Highest-value commercial books to buy

- **Patrick Olivelle**, *King, Governance, and Law in Ancient India: Kautilya's Arthasastra* (2013) — Best single upgrade for the Chanakya end of the list.
- **Joseph Bergin**, *The Rise of Richelieu* (1991) — Best targeted upgrade for Richelieu.
- **Wolfram Siemann**, *Metternich: Strategist and Visionary* (2019) — Major modern study strengthening the diplomacy tier.
- **Andrew Mango**, *Ataturk* (1999) — Anchor modern biography for Atatürk.
- **Diarmaid MacCulloch**, *Thomas Cromwell* (2018) — Probably the strongest single upgrade for Cromwell.
- **Robert A. Caro**, *Means of Ascent* (1990) — Best purchase for LBJ as a legislative and power strategist.
- **Jean Monnet**, *Memoirs* (1978 English edition) — Primary memoir evidence for Monnet's strategic role.
- **Lee Kuan Yew**, *From Third World to First* (2000) — Core self-interpretation of Singaporean statecraft.
- **David Axelrod**, *Believer: My Forty Years in Politics* (2015) — Useful because modern campaign-consultant tier is thinner in open sources.
- **Philip Gould**, *The Unfinished Revolution* (1998 / expanded 2011) — Serious upgrade for British centre-left strategy.
- **Alastair Campbell**, *The Blair Years* (2010) — Dense primary-source window into message discipline and government communications.
- **Peter Mandelson**, *The Third Man* (2010) — Core insider document for New Labour repositioning.
- **Joseph Napolitan**, *The Election Game and How to Win It* (1972) — Foundational text of modern political consulting.
- **Edward Bernays**, *Propaganda* (modern reprint recommended) — Foundational persuasion text for modern campaign strategy.

## Second-tier commercial books

- **James Carville**, *We're Right, They're Wrong* (1996) — Useful late-20th-century Democratic combat messaging source.
- **Karl Rove**, *Courage and Consequence* (2010) — Insider account of the Bush-Rove machine.

## Databases with unusually high payoff

- **JSTOR institutional access** — Higher marginal value than many additional trade books for this project.
- **ProQuest Historical Newspapers** — Especially valuable for Mark Hanna, Parnell, Walpole, Samuel Adams, party machines, and late-20th-century strategists.
